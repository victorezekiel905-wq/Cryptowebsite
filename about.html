About SwiftCipher

Five years of building the most trusted crypto investment infrastructure in the market. Meet the team and story behind the platform.

Est. 2021

From a Vision to a Market Leader

SwiftCipher was founded in 2021 by a team of veteran financial technologists and blockchain engineers who recognized a critical gap in the market: retail investors deserved the same institutional-quality tools that hedge funds and proprietary trading desks had access to.

What began as a small, high-conviction team of seven has grown into a globally recognized platform trusted by over 48,000 active investors across 180+ countries. Our infrastructure now processes billions in monthly trading volume with sub-millisecond execution.

Five years later, our founding principle remains unchanged: democratize institutional-grade crypto investing for everyone.

📅 Company History

5 Years of Market Excellence

Q1 2021 — Foundation
SwiftCipher is Born

Founded in London by a 7-person team of ex-Goldman Sachs technologists and blockchain engineers. Seed funding of $1.2M secured. Core matching engine architecture begins development. Mission established: democratize institutional crypto investing.

Q3 2021 — Beta Launch
Private Beta & First 1,000 Investors

Private beta launched to an invite-only cohort of 500 investors. Cold storage vault infrastructure deployed with $50M capacity. First institutional partnerships established. Platform receives its first independent security audit — zero critical vulnerabilities.

2022 — Global Expansion
Scaling to 50+ Countries

Public launch in Q1 2022. Expanded to 50 countries with localized support. Series A funding of $8.5M closed. Singapore office opened to serve Asia-Pacific markets. AML/KYC compliance framework certified. Assets under management surpassed $100M.

2023 — Product Innovation
AI Portfolio Engine Launch

Launched the proprietary AI Portfolio Insights engine — trained on 5TB of market data. Introduced automated portfolio rebalancing and risk-adjusted return modeling. Dubai operations center opened. User base surpassed 20,000 active investors. AUM crossed $800M.

2024 — Market Leadership
$2B AUM Milestone & 40,000 Investors

Crossed $2 billion in assets under management. Launched advanced charting suite and tax reporting tools. 40,000+ active investors across 150 countries. Introduced institutional accounts and OTC desk for high-net-worth clients. Named "Best Emerging Crypto Platform" by FinTech Weekly.

2025–2026 — Present
48,200+ Investors · $2.4B AUM

Continuing to grow with a focus on advanced DeFi integration, multi-chain support, and enterprise-grade institutional products. New mobile app launched with biometric authentication. Expanding to 30 new markets in Africa and Southeast Asia. The mission continues.
